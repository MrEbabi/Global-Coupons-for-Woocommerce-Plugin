=== Plugin Details ===
Global Coupons are the customizated WooCommerce coupons that has several restriction options and regarding to them available for the customers. 
Customers can inspect the Active or Deactive Global Coupons on the My Account - Coupons part. 
If a coupon is active for customer, she/he can use it if she/he has at least one item in the cart. 
The restrictions are: First Order, Number of Orders, Amount of Orders, Special For You, Number of Reviews and Activate Date Interval.
Use of Global Coupons at the Admin Panel is explained with examples and regular inputs in the Plugin menu.
